Where we get our data

Project summary

The Northern Kenya Grassland Carbon Project is registered with Verra as VCS 1468, with Northern Rangelands Trust as proponent. It covers 1,993,075 hectares across 14 community conservancies, under methodology VM0032 (Agricultural Land Management). The first crediting period runs from 2013 to 2042. Verra lists the project as On Hold — see the notification letter on the registry. For full official details, issuance records and buffer pool figures, see the Verra Registry (VCS 1468).
