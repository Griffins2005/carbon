Project summary

The Northern Kenya Grassland Carbon Project (NRT) is registered as VCS 1468, with Northern Rangelands Trust as proponent. It covers 1,993,075 hectares across 14 community conservancies. We also track Komaza (VCS 2623), Boomitra Kenya (VCS 3340), and KCSA (VCS 5451). For official details and issuance records, see the Verra Registry.

Hectare figure and conservancy count: NRT (VCS 1468) from Verra Registry and NRT project documents.
